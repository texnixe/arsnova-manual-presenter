Title: Present Preparation Questions
----
URL-Key: present-preparation-questions
----
Cat: JiTT
----
Steps:


1:
	Subheader: 
	Action: Click into the Session dropdown menu in the header bar and select a session from the list of available session. 
	Details: &rarr; The session is loaded.

2:
	Subheader: 
	Action: Select **Mode &rarr; Preparation (JiTT)**  from the dropdown menu in the header bar..
	Details:>
		&rarr; All available preparation questions are shown in the lecturer pane.
	

		To start with the second Peer Instruction round, you can set the timer to give the students some minutes to discuss the questions with their peer.
		
3:
	Subheader: Set and Start Countdown Timer
	Action: Click **View &rarr; Countdown-Timer**.
	Details: >
		&rarr; The dialog for setting the timer opens.
4:
	Subheader: 
		Action: Set the desired time for the Countdown Timer and click **Start**. 
	Details: >
		&rarr; The Countdown-Timer starts.
5:
	Subheader: 
	Action: The Countdown-Timer is displayed full window as an overlay over the session.
	Details: &rarr; Once the timer has expired, the  display begins to flash red.
		
6:
	Subheader: 
	Action: Hit the ESC key to close the overlay.
	Details: >
		The overlay is closed.	

7:
	Subheader: 
	Action: You are back in session view.
	Details: >
		As a next step, you can now present the answers of the first round of Peer Instruction.
